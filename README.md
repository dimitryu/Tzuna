# MealTrack - Daily Nutrition & Meal Logger

A streamlined application designed for daily meal registration to support a healthy lifestyle. This app helps you keep track of your nutrition goals with a focus on privacy and simplicity.

## 🚀 Features
- **Daily Meal Logging:** Quickly record your meals throughout the day.
- **7-Day Free Trial:** Full access to all features for the first week.
- **Subscription Models:** Choose between a monthly plan (29 ILS) or a one-time lifetime payment (149 ILS).
- **Privacy First:** Data is stored locally on your device with secure synchronization to a private Firebase backend.
- **Zero Third-Party Tracking:** No Google Analytics or Crashlytics involved.
- **Full Data Control:** Users can delete their entire account and data directly from the app at any time.

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- **Database & Auth:** Google Firebase (Private Instance).
- **Architecture:** Local-first storage with cloud synchronization.
